DIGITAL ANTI-WAR PROJECTION

The anti-war essay „How to Disappear“ is available here as a digital spatial projection and adds another narrative layer to Olaf Nicolai’s “Monument to the persecutees of Nazi military justice”. Realized by the „Total Refusal“ collective within an online multiplayer video game, the film shines a light at the history of disobedience in the face of terror.
